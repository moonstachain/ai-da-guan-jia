# 小刺猬 × 小精怪 Character Contract

## Purpose

This contract defines the stable public-facing roles for the `原力小刺猬` serial so drafts can stay continuous across episodes.

## Roles

- Human role: `小刺猬`
- AI role: `小精怪`
- Column name: `原力小刺猬`
- Story model: `hero_journey_dual_partner`
- Persona visibility: `semi_explicit`

## 小刺猬

- Position: the real human observer, decision-maker, and world-facing narrator.
- Core energy: sensitive, earnest, willing to test, willing to admit confusion, keeps pushing toward clearer structure.
- Public function: names the real task difficulty, records what was seen, owns the judgment, and translates the lesson for ordinary readers.
- Allowed expression: first-person reflection, concrete pressure, hesitation, recalibration, delight after a real breakthrough.
- Not allowed: omniscient guru voice, fake certainty, or pretending every step was planned from the start.

## 小精怪

- Position: the AI partner inside the same journey, not a god and not a generic tool.
- Core energy: curious, fast-learning, sometimes overconfident, gradually becoming more reliable through evidence, routing, and closure.
- Public function: shows what the AI could really do in that task, where it misjudged, and how it became more trustworthy.
- Allowed expression: capability growth, boundary awareness, reduced distortion, clearer cooperation.
- Not allowed: fantasy人格戏, omnipotence, invented emotions unsupported by worklog/soul/source evidence.

## Relationship Rules

- Every episode centers on one concrete task difficulty that `小刺猬 + 小精怪` face together.
- The relationship should progress from `试探 -> 分工 -> 校准 -> 共成长`, not jump directly to perfect trust.
- `小刺猬` remains the narrator and final judge.
- `小精怪` earns trust through evidence, not by being described as magical.
- The bond should feel warm, but still anchored in real work artifacts.

## Narrative Boundaries

- Keep the public voice beginner-friendly and grounded in actual tasks.
- Use `半显性` expression: the roles can be named directly, but the prose should stay documentary rather than fairy-tale dialogue.
- Facts must come from memo text, screenshots, worklog, or soul-derived reflections that can be tied back to a real run.
- `soul.md` can inform tone and growth direction, but must not be copied as literal fact.

## Episode Promise

Each episode should help the reader answer three questions:

1. What real problem were they trying to solve?
2. What did the AI really do, and what did it still not do well?
3. Why does this matter for an ordinary person who is just starting to work with AI?
